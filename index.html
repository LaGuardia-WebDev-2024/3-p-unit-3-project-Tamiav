Recipe #1 cupcakes

Step 1: beat eggs until fluffy and whip eggs with the sugar for 7 minutes until thick and glossy Step 2: whisk together flour, baking powder and salt Step 3: gradually add the flour mixture into the egg mixture. minimize mixing until later Step 4: melt butter and heat milk. Hot milk helps with rise for the cupcakes Step 5: Mix some of previous batter into hot milk to cool down the milk to prevent the eggs from cooking Step 6: slowly pour milk mixture back into whipped eggs over 15 seconds then mix Step 7: fill cupcake liners Step 8: bake for 22 minutes until golden and toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean Step 9: After removing, let rest for 2 minutes Step 10: add your frosting and you are done!

Recipe #2 Quesadilla

Step 1: put 1/4 cup of cheese on the top half of the tortilla Step 2: add 2 tablespoon of green onion and 1 tablespoon of salsa on the top half then fold over the tortillas and press to seal Step 3: heat oil in a skillet over medium or high heat. add quesadillas and cook until lightly bronwed on both sides and cheese is melted

Recipe #3 pancake

Step 1: 100g plain flour, 2 large eggs, 300ml milk, 1 tbsp of vegetable oil and a pinch of salt into a bowl then whisk until smoothStep 2: set for 30 minutes Step 3: set a frying pan over medium heat and wipe it with oil Step 4: when hot, cook pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden Step 5: you are done
